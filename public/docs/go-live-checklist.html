Go‑Live Checklist

Use this 10‑minute checklist to prepare your venue for launch.

1. Business & Tables

  • Business profile (name, phone, address) completed
  • Tables created with correct numbers and capacities
  • Optional: Seats configured (if using seat selection)

2. Menu

  • Menu items and categories created
  • Prices and availability verified
  • Popular items added to spotlight (optional)

Note: Menu is managed in Dashboard → Menu, not in QR Builder.

3. QR Codes

  • Generate designs in Dashboard → QR Builder
  • For Table QR, select the exact table in Details
  • Print PNGs or download SVG for professional print

4. Kitchen Display (KDS)

  • Open Dashboard → Kitchen and confirm orders appear
  • Test status flow: Pending → Accepted → Preparing → Almost Ready → Ready → Served
  • Try customer messages: “Please wait”, “Item unavailable”, “Almost ready”, “Ready”

5. Payments

  • Test at least one real or sandbox payment
  • Verify receipt/confirmation is received
  • Confirm refunds flow (if applicable)

6. Staff Training

  • Waiter calls monitored and resolved
  • Kitchen status transitions practiced
  • Fallback process for offline scenarios agreed

7. Launch

  • Place printed QR codes on tables/stands
  • Make first 3–5 real orders to validate end‑to‑end
  • Monitor Dashboard for the first service hour

Need help? Contact support or your onboarding manager for a 30‑minute guided session.
