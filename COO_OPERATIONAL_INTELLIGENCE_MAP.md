## Category 1: Service Delivery Quality

**Executive Value**: CRITICAL (95/100)

**Why**: Direct impact on brand, revenue, customer retention

---

### Signal 1.1: Service Response Time

**Definition**: Time from customer request to service delivery

**Measurement**:
- Order to delivery time (restaurants)
- Check-in to room ready time (hotels)
- Request to fulfillment time (all)

**Thresholds**:
- CRITICAL: >2x standard
- WARNING: >1.5x standard
- HEALTHY: Within standard

**Decision Support**:

**Executive Value**: 90/100

## Intelligence vs. KPI Distinction

### KPIs (CEO/CFO Focus)

**Examples**:
- Revenue
- Profit margin
- Customer acquisition cost
- Market share

**Characteristics**:
- Financial outcomes
- Strategic metrics
- Lagging indicators
- Monthly/quarterly review

---

### Operational Intelligence (COO Focus)

**Examples**:
- Shift coverage health
- Incident frequency
- Queue depth
- Service response time

**Characteristics**:
- Execution quality
- Operational metrics
- Leading indicators
- Real-time to daily review

**Key Difference**: Intelligence shows **how well we're executing**, KPIs show **what we achieved**
