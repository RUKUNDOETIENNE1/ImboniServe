## 📚 Cultural Sensitivity Notes

### **Historical Context**
Rwanda has a sensitive history regarding ethnic categorization. Using terms like "ubwoko" (type/ethnicity) in any context, even business, can be uncomfortable or inappropriate.

### **Best Practices**
1. ✅ Use "Icyiciro" (category/class) instead of "Ubwoko" (type)
2. ✅ Use "Ikigo" (institution) for business organizations
3. ✅ Use "Umuryango" only for family/association contexts
4. ✅ Keep business terminology neutral and professional

### **Professional Terminology**
- **Contact Category** = `Icyiciro cya contact`
- **Organization** = `Ikigo`
- **Institution** = `Ikigo`
- **Business** = `Ubucuruzi`
- **Company** = `Isosiyete`
