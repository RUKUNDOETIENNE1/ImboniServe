## Global-by-Design Alignment Review

### Core Principle
ImboniServe is a **global hospitality platform** with configurable localization.

All Homepage content must distinguish between:
1. **Core Platform** (universal capabilities)
2. **Localization** (configurable per deployment)
3. **Deployment Availability** (what's currently live)
