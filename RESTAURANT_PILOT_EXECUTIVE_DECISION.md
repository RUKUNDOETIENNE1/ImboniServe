## Blockers Analysis

### BLOCKER #1: Menu Builder UI Confusion

**Impact**: 🔴 CRITICAL (100% of users)

**Problem**: AI Menu Builder shown first (locked), manual option shown second (buried)

**User Reaction**: "Do I need 20 clients first?"

**Support Tickets**: 2-3 per 5 restaurants (40-60%)

**Fix**: Swap div order (manual first, AI second)

**Effort**: 15 minutes

**Status**: ❌ NOT FIXED (blocking deployment)

---

### BLOCKER #2: Missing Role Descriptions

**Impact**: 🟠 HIGH (40% of multi-user restaurants)

**Problem**: No tooltips explaining role permissions

**User Reaction**: "What's the difference between WAITER and CASHIER?"

**Support Tickets**: 1-2 per 5 restaurants (20-40%)

**Fix**: Add role description tooltips

**Effort**: 2 hours

**Status**: ❌ NOT FIXED (blocking deployment)
